About

Crafting content that speaks, visuals that stick, and ideas that sell.

Hi, I’m Palak Pathak — a content creator, aspiring advertising specialist, and passionate storyteller. With a deep love for voiceovers, reels, campaigns, and community-driven stories, I turn ideas into content that connects and converts.

From scripting and producing digital campaigns at Delta Crest Media, to engaging audiences as an RJ at Lakecity Voice, I blend strategy with storytelling across every medium.
